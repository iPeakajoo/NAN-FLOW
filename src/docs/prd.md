1. Project Overview
Project Name

NAN FLOW

Tagline

Explore Beyond Seasons. Empower Local Communities.

One Sentence

An AI-powered tourism platform that helps distribute travelers to local communities during the low/green season while providing measurable impact through a government dashboard.
